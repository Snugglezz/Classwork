Header, Main and footer tags

These 3 tags both section the webpage into 3 sections and they all have their own unique places, Main is for everything and all the information, the header is where the title and where the section links may be, and the footer is where links, contacts...etc all the important information connected ot the programmer or writer.

Section tags

The section tag is a place in the webpage that may hold chapters, introductions, information, all thats in a section of an article can be what is inside an section tag.

Article tag

very simular to an section tag but the major difference is that this tag can just be by itself and it can have multiple tags of it. basically another form of displaying information like blogs, news, forum, and user comments.

Aside tag

simular to a section tag but the diffference is that the information is sections off to the side of the webpage and not in the center, bottom or the top of the page.

Image, Audio, and Video tags

they all work together but they can all work seperate. the image tag is for images/ to place images, audio is used to embed audio and video is to show moving images that may consist of the two previous tags.

Nav tag

The Nav tag is where you can find all youre links to other websites and or other files to other code. nav is short for navigation.

Source tag

the source tag pairs with image, audio, video and other tags that converse with the media. it can allow you to past a media outlet of your choosing from a browser.
